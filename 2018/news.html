2018-09-26
 We request that all the TOP X finalists record a 1- to 2-minute video show to introduce your team members and the program you wrote. These video shows will be played in the Award Ceremony at ICCAD. This is a valuable chance for you to disclose your key ideas and algorithms to different professionals in this most well-established CAD conference. Certainly, you may also tell us how you get the inspiration and introduce your team members in the video show. You may even introduce yourselves in a creative way to let all ICCAD participants from the industry/academia know you better.
The video show should be recorded in the MPEG format. Please email the MPEG file to cad.contest.iccad@gmail.com by October 12th, 2018 with the following title: “ICCAD2018_Video Show of Problem X (cadaXXX)”.
Thank you very much for your attention.
