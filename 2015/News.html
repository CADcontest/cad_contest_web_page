  • Cadence Design Systems will provide dinner in ICCAD-2015 CAD Contest special session for all CAD Contest attendees and participants. You are invited to attend ICCAD-2015 CAD Contest special session!!-2015/10/04

  • The CAD contests at ICCAD and associated benchmark suites have been instrumental in advancing the state-of-the-art in EDA. Additionally, they have fostered productive industry-academia collaboration. In its fourth year, the 2015 CAD contest is among the largest worldwide EDA contests, attracting 112 teams from 12 regions/countries. In 2015 ICCAD CAD Contest Special Session (Session 10B), we will present the three contest problems, releases the associated benchmarks, and announces the winners. The special session will also provide a venue for the top-performing teams to showcase their key ideas via short video presentations. Please refer to the link: http://iccad.com/event_details?id=196-10-B You are invited to attend this special session!!

  • We request that all the TOP X teams of our contest record a 1- to 2-minute video show to introduce your team members and the program you wrote. These video shows will be played in the Award Ceremony. This is a valuable chance for you to disclose your key ideas and algorithms to different professionals in this most well-established CAD conference. Certainly, you may also tell us how you get the inspiration and introduce your team members in the video show. You may even introduce yourselves in a creative way and so let the rest of the industry/academia remember your names/faces. The show should be recorded in the form of MPEG. Please send the file to cad.contest.iccad@gmail.com by October 14, 2015 and remember to add “ICCAD2015_Video Show of Problem (A, B or C)” to the subject line of that e-mail.-2015/09/24
